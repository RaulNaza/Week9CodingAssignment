My Trip To Machu Picchu!

My counsins and I planned this trip about a year ago and it finally happened! The total flight time was awful (overnight flight) but well worth it.
For the first time we got to see one of the greatest Wonders of the World. To be honest, all the photos and videos you find online do NOT do it justice.
My cousins and I had an amazing time walking thorugh the city and would definetely recommend you visit!
